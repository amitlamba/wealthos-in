## What is Wealth OS?

Wealth OS helps you define your financial goals, track progress, and achieve them. You set goals (e.g. savings, investments, milestones), give them targets and timelines, and track how you're doing.

## Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry-standard security and encryption. Your goals and progress data are stored securely.

## How do I get started?

Visit [app.wealthos.in](https://app.wealthos.in) to sign up or sign in. After creating an account, you can define your goals and start tracking progress.

## Is there a mobile app?

Our web app works on mobile browsers. A dedicated mobile app may be available in the future.

## Who can I contact for support?

Use the in-app support or contact options available at [app.wealthos.in](https://app.wealthos.in).
